Boolean Search Tips

AND

Include two terms by separating those terms with the uppercase word “AND”. For example: Sales AND Director

OR

Include one or more terms by separating those terms with the uppercase word “OR”. For example: Sales operations OR Sales Ops

NOT

Exclude a particular term by including the uppercase word “NOT” immediately before it. For example: NOT director

“ ”

Search for an exact phrase or words with punctuation by enclosing them in quotation marks. For example: “human resource manager”

()

Combine terms and modifiers by enclosing them in parentheses for a more complex search. For example: Marketing AND (B2B OR B2C)
